All about Calico Cats

Fun Facts about Calico Cats

  • Most Calico cats are female.
  • A similar cat is the Tortoiseshell.
  • Calicos might be good luck; they are know as money cats.
  • Maryland adopts Calico as their ofiical cat.
  • Calico coats consist of butterscotch, vanilla, and chocolate swirls.
  • They are affectionate and fiercly independent.
  • Can live to 12- 20 years old.

Care Needs of Calico Cats

Male Calico cats are prone to behavioral problems, obesity, cognitive and development issues in addition to facing sterilization. Calico cats need to be brushed weekly. The shorthaired needs to be brushed less of course! All cats need exercis and enrichment regardless of breed. Make sure to take your cat to the vet regualry and keep up to date with vaccines.
